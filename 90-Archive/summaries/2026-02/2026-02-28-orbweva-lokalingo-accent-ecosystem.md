# Orbweva → LokaLingo → Accent Language Ecosystem

## Overview

Three interconnected platforms built by the same team, forming a complete business ecosystem from AI automation to language education delivery.

---

## 1. Orbweva (orbweva.com)

**Tagline**: AI That Grows Your Business

**Product**: ORBWEVA AER™ — AI automation platform
- **A**cquire — Lead generation
- **E**ngage — Daily content automation
- **R**etain — 24/7 customer support

**Pricing**: From $500/month

**Purpose**: B2B AI automation service for customer lifecycle management

---

## 2. LokaLingo (lokalingo.com)

**Tagline**: Where Conversations Become Curriculum

**Product**: AI-powered platform for language educators
- Converts real conversations into learning curriculum
- Browser-based, no installation required
- Includes "The Living Textbook" — personalized, adaptive learning journey

**Pricing**: $6/seat, no commissions

**Purpose**: EdTech platform enabling educators to create personalized language learning experiences

---

## 3. Accent Language (accent-language.com)

**Tagline**: Accent Aspire Communication Coaching

**Product**: Neurolanguage coaching service
- ICF-accredited coach: Ryan Ahamer
- Target: Mid-career professionals in Japan and Korea
- Methods: Neurolanguage Coaching, Culture Map (Erin Meyer), Quiet Leadership (David Rock)
- Platform: Powered by LokaLingo's "The Living Textbook"

**Key Features**:
- 5 C's Framework (Concrete Requirements, Clear Targets, Coaching Conversations, Connecting the Brain, Conquering Barriers)
- 12-week coaching quarters
- Private and group sessions via Zoom
- Corporate clients: Symantec, Veeva, Treasury Wine Estates, Johnson & Johnson

**Purpose**: Live example of LokaLingo in production — a fully operational coaching business
