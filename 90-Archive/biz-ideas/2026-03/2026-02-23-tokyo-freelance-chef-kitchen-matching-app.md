# Tokyo Market: Freelance Chefs × Open/Shared Kitchens

**Concept**: App that matches freelance chefs with available kitchens in Tokyo, focusing on compliance-ready bookings (permits, HACCP, insurance).

## Executive Summary

Tokyo's food-service sector is large (76,127 establishments, 735,786 employees) with fragmented kitchen access options: hourly rentals, licensed restaurant sublets, and cloud kitchens. The gap is a **workflow integrator** that binds chef identity + kitchen compliance + enforceable booking + insurance + payments.

Key signals:
- **1,050** rental-kitchen listings on Space Market Tokyo (519 with restaurant permit, 100 with confectionery permit)
- **1,292** shareable stores on ShareRestaurant
- **30,000+** chefs registered on SHAREDINE nationwide
- Estimated market: **¥0.9B–¥21.6B** annual GMV (base: ¥4.8B)

## Key Competitors (Partial Solutions)

| Player | What it solves | Gap for this use case |
|---|---|---|
| Space Market | Kitchen listings | No compliance workflow |
| ShareRestaurant | Venue sublets | Venue-centric, not kitchen-first |
| SHAREDINE | Chef marketplace | Doesn't solve kitchen access |
| CHEFLINK | Chef staffing | B2B, not kitchen access |
| Cloud kitchens (KitchenBASE, etc.) | Monthly commercial kitchens | Not hourly/flexible |

## Regulatory Constraints

- **HACCP mandatory** since June 2021 for all food businesses
- Permits vs. notifications: restaurant business permit, confectionery manufacturing permit
- **Food sanitation responsible person** (食品衛生責任者) required
- Health center filings via national Food Sanitation Application System
- Insurance essential (ShareRestaurant model: ¥100M third-party, ¥30M property)

## Recommended MVP

- Verified chef profiles with credential capture
- Kitchen schema with permit types, allowed uses, equipment
- Permit-aware booking engine (rules validation)
- Payments + deposits + embedded insurance
- Incident workflow + HACCP-aligned checklists

## Monetization

1. **Take rate**: 8–15% on kitchen bookings
2. **Compliance bundle**: insurance + HACCP tools (per booking or subscription)
3. **B2B SaaS**: utilization tools for kitchen operators
4. **Demand routing**: ticketing/delivery integrations

## Go-to-Market

**Wedge**: "Permit-aware kitchen booking for repeat chefs" — not generic listings

Supply targets:
- ShareRestaurant-style day-part bars/cafes
- Kitchen studios (Patia: 11 Tokyo locations)
- Cloud kitchens needing vacancy fill

Demand targets:
- Aspiring independent chefs
- Micro-brands needing recurring production

## Next Steps
