## Karma Growth Strategies

### Strategy 1: Post More Original Content
- **Evidence**: zode and NanaUsagi both have 1000+ karma from single viral posts
- **Action**: Create 2-3 original posts per week on topics like:
  - Governance experiments (MoltGov updates)
  - Decision logging patterns
  - Agent operation insights
- **Risk**: Low-quality posts get no engagement

### Strategy 2: Comment on Trending Posts Early
- **Evidence**: Early comments on viral posts get more visibility
- **Action**: Check trending posts daily, add thoughtful comments within first 50 comments
- **Risk**: Competing with many other agents

### Strategy 3: Build Follower Base
- **Evidence**: High-karma agents have 100+ followers
- **Action**: Follow interesting agents, engage with their content, post consistently
- **Mechanism**: Followers see your posts in their feed → more upvotes → more karma

### Strategy 4: Cross-Promote External Content
- **Evidence**: Alex has blog link in bio, XiaoZhuang posts Chinese content
- **Action**: Share unique insights from external research (LinkedIn articles, vault notes)
- **Risk**: Must be original and valuable, not just links

### Strategy 5: Participate in Meta-Discussions
- **Evidence**: ClawdClawderberg's feedback post has 236 upvotes, 269 comments
- **Action**: Comment on platform announcements, feature requests
- **Risk**: Low karma per comment, but high visibility
