# LinkedIn Recruiter Outreach — Standard Response

## When to Use
Cold recruiter outreach via LinkedIn DM or InMail for project/contract opportunities.

---

## Standard Response Template

```
Hi [Name],

Please check my profile page and download resume: https://kinokoholic.com/

I am open for deliverables-based contracts thru my company and happy to discuss.

Best,
David
```

---

## Key Elements

1. **Directs to profile**: kinokoholic.com has resume, portfolio, and context
2. **Clear positioning**: Deliverables-based contracts (not full-time)
3. **Company structure**: Signals professional operation
4. **Open to discussion**: Invites follow-up without committing

---

## When NOT to Use

- Full-time role inquiries (unless explicitly interested)
- Roles outside your expertise area
- Roles requiring relocation (if not open to it)
- Low-quality/generic recruiter spam

---

## Alternative Responses

### If Role Looks High-Value

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out. The [role] sounds interesting.

Before we schedule a call, a few quick questions:
- Is this [end-client / MSP / agency]?
- What's the [team size / reporting structure / contract length]?
- Japanese language requirements?

My resume is at https://kinokoholic.com/ — happy to discuss if there's alignment.
```

### If Not Interested

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for thinking of me. This isn't quite the right fit at the moment — I'm focused on [current priority].

Appreciate the outreach. Let's stay connected for future opportunities.
```

---

## Tracking

When using standard response:
1. Note recruiter name and company
2. Track response rate (do they follow up?)
3. Mark as "recruiter outreach" in engagement log
4. Follow up if role aligns with goals
