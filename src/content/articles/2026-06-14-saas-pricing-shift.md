### The Seat Pricing Scam is Over

For years, SaaS companies have milked businesses with seat-based pricing. More users = more money, regardless of actual value. But AI agents are making this model look ridiculous. A single AI system can now perform the work of dozens of users—automating workflows, generating insights, completing tasks independently. Why pay for 50 seats when one AI can do the work of 50 people?

This isn't just theoretical. Companies are already voting with their wallets:

- **SaaS pricing is shifting from seats to usage** as AI becomes the primary value driver
- **Open-source alternatives are gaining traction** as companies seek sovereignty over their tools
- **Hybrid pricing models are becoming standard** as traditional vendors scramble to adapt
