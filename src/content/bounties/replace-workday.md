Workday HCM costs $600-1000/employee/year. That's $30,000-50,000/year for a 50-person company. The UI is confusing, customization requires expensive consultants, and you're locked into their release cycle.

We need a simpler HR system that handles the basics — employee records, time off, documents — without the enterprise complexity.
