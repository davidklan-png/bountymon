ServiceNow ITSM costs $1200+/seat/year. That's $12,000/year for a 10-person IT team. The platform is powerful but overkill for most orgs — and the complexity means you often need consultants to configure it.

We need a focused ITSM tool that handles incidents, changes, and assets without the enterprise sprawl.
