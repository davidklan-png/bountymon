Open-source Datadog alternative. Metrics, traces, and logs in a single pane of glass — built on OpenTelemetry.

## Why it works

- **Unified observability**: Metrics, traces, and logs together
- **OpenTelemetry-native**: Industry-standard instrumentation
- **Alert management**: Built-in PagerDuty/Slack integrations
- **No per-seat pricing**: Self-host at any scale
- **ClickHouse-powered**: Fast queries over massive datasets

## What you lose

- Datadog's 600+ integrations out of the box
- Managed infrastructure (you run it yourself)
- Some AI-powered anomaly detection features
