Full self-hosted cloud storage with collaboration features. Nextcloud provides file sync, calendar, contacts, mail, and a full office suite — replacing multiple SaaS subscriptions with a single deployment.

## Why it works

- **Feature parity**: File sync, sharing, online editing, mobile apps
- **Extensible**: 400+ apps in the Nextcloud App Store
- **Enterprise-ready**: Used by German federal government, CERN, and thousands of organizations
- **Active development**: 20+ releases per year, massive community

## What you lose

- Zero-maintenance cloud convenience (you own the ops now)
- Native integrations with Google/Microsoft ecosystems
