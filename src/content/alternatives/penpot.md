The first open-source design and prototyping platform. Figma alternative that respects your privacy and gives you full control.

## Why it works

- **SVG-native**: Your designs are real SVG files, not proprietary formats
- **Real-time collaboration**: Multiplayer editing like Figma
- **Design systems**: Tokens, components, and shared libraries
- **No vendor lock-in**: Export to any format, migrate anywhere
- **Self-hosted**: Keep your design assets on your infrastructure

## What you lose

- Figma's extensive plugin ecosystem
- Some advanced prototyping features
- Native mobile app (web-only)
