The ultimate open-source chat platform. Team messaging, livechat, video calls, and customer support — all in one self-hosted package.

## Why it works

- **Omnichannel**: Chat, video, voice, email, and social media in one place
- **Federation**: Connect multiple Rocket.Chat servers securely
- **Livechat widget**: Customer support built-in, no separate tool needed
- **Video conferencing**: Native video and screen sharing via WebRTC
- **Extensive marketplace**: Apps, bots, and integrations

## What you lose

- The polished UX of Slack's client apps
- Some advanced search and threading features
