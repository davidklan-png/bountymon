The open-source CRM built for the modern era. Clean UI, customizable, and no per-seat licensing.

## Why it works

- **Beautiful UI**: Modern, clean interface that doesn't feel enterprise-clunky
- **Flexible data model**: Customize objects and fields to match your workflow
- **GraphQL API**: Developer-friendly integrations
- **Automation**: Workflow automation and email sequences
- **Free forever**: Self-host with unlimited seats

## What you lose

- Salesforce's massive AppExchange ecosystem
- Native marketing automation (connects to external tools)
- Enterprise territory management features
