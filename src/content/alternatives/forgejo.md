Community-controlled Gitea fork. Same great experience, community-governed future.

## Why it works

- **Federation support**: Star repos across Forgejo instances
- **Community-governed**: No corporate takeover risk
- **Gitea-compatible**: Drop-in replacement, same features
- **Lightweight**: Runs on anything from a Pi to a datacenter
- **Actions CI/CD**: GitHub Actions-compatible workflows

## What you lose

- Smaller ecosystem than Gitea (but growing)
- Some third-party Gitea themes/plugins may need updates
