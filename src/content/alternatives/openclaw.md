Personal AI assistant platform that unifies multiple LLM providers into a single self-hosted interface. Stop paying per-seat for every AI tool.

## Why it works

- **Multi-model**: Use OpenAI, Anthropic, local models — one interface
- **Vibe-coded**: Built with AI-assisted development, proving the concept
- **Data sovereignty**: Your prompts and data never leave your infrastructure
- **Cost effective**: Pay API rates instead of per-seat enterprise pricing

## What you lose

- Polished consumer UX of commercial offerings
- Automatic model updates and fine-tuning
