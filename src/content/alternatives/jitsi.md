Secure, open-source video conferencing. No accounts needed, end-to-end encryption, runs on your infrastructure.

## Why it works

- **No accounts**: Guests join with a link, no signup required
- **E2E encryption**: Optional end-to-end encryption for sensitive calls
- **Scale**: Supports hundreds of participants
- **Feature-complete**: Screen sharing, breakout rooms, recording, live streaming
- **Embeddable**: Add video to your app with Jitsi Meet SDK

## What you lose

- Zoom's polished enterprise admin console
- Some AI-powered features (transcription, translation)
- Native calendar integrations
