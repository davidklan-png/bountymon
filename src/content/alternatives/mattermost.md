Open-source Slack alternative built for developers. Self-hosted team messaging with enterprise-grade security and compliance.

## Why it works

- **Slack-compatible**: Import channels, users, and history from Slack
- **DevOps integrations**: Native integrations with Jira, GitHub, GitLab, Jupyter
- **Enterprise security**: SSO, MFA, data retention policies, audit logs
- **High-performance**: Handles thousands of concurrent users
- **Extensible**: Plugin system, webhook APIs, custom slash commands

## What you lose

- Slack's massive third-party app marketplace
- Some of the polish and UI refinements
- Huddles / native voice/video (requires separate integration)
