Self-hosted photo and video backup with AI-powered search, face recognition, and a beautiful mobile app that rivals Google Photos.

## Why it works

- **Mobile-first**: Native iOS and Android apps with automatic backup
- **AI features**: Face recognition, object detection, smart search
- **Fast**: Hardware-accelerated ML, responsive web UI
- **Active community**: One of the fastest-growing self-hosted projects

## What you lose

- Google's unlimited processing power for ML features
- Seamless integration with Android/iOS photo systems
