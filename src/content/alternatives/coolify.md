Open-source Heroku alternative. Deploy anything — static sites, Node.js apps, Docker containers, databases — on your own servers.

## Why it works

- **Push-to-deploy**: Git-based deployments like Heroku
- **Auto SSL**: Let's Encrypt certificates automatically
- **Any server**: AWS, DigitalOcean, Hetzner, or your basement
- **Databases**: PostgreSQL, MySQL, Redis, MongoDB one-click deploy
- **Zero lock-in**: Standard Docker under the hood

## What you lose

- Vercel's edge network and ISR
- Heroku's add-on marketplace
- Some auto-scaling conveniences
