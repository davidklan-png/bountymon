Local smart home automation that works with everything. 2000+ integrations, runs locally, no cloud dependency.

## Why it works

- **Local-first**: Everything runs on your network — no cloud required
- **Universal**: Works with Zigbee, Z-Wave, WiFi, Bluetooth, Matter, and more
- **Powerful automation**: Visual automation editor + YAML for power users
- **Massive community**: One of the largest open-source home automation projects

## What you lose

- Plug-and-play simplicity of commercial hubs
- Voice assistant integrations (though you can self-host those too)
