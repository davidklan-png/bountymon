Privacy-focused web analytics that gives you all the insights you need without tracking your visitors. A single script tag, no cookies, GDPR-compliant out of the box.

## Why it works

- **Privacy-first**: No cookies, no personal data collection
- **Lightweight**: < 1KB script vs Google Analytics' 45KB+
- **Simple**: Dashboard shows what matters without the bloat
- **Open source**: Self-host or use their cloud offering

## What you lose

- Advanced funnel analysis and cohort reports
- Integration with the Google advertising ecosystem
