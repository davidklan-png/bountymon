Open-source product analytics. Event tracking, session replays, feature flags, and experimentation — all self-hosted.

## Why it works

- **All-in-one**: Analytics, session replay, feature flags, experiments
- **SQL access**: Query your data directly with SQL
- **Self-hosted**: Keep user data on your infrastructure
- **Generous free tier**: 1M events/month on cloud, unlimited self-hosted
- **Event pipeline**: Transform and route events with HogQL

## What you lose

- The polished onboarding experience of Amplitude
- Some pre-built analytics templates
